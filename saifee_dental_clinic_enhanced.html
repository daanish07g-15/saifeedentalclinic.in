Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Is root canal treatment really painless?
+
Yes — modern RCT with proper anaesthesia is no more uncomfortable than a filling. Dr. Rangwala uses the latest rotary techniques and takes time to ensure you're fully numb before proceeding. Hundreds of our reviews specifically mention how pain-free the experience was.
How much do treatments cost?
+
We believe in complete cost transparency. You'll always be told the full cost before any treatment begins — no surprise charges. Pricing is competitive and honest. Call us for specific estimates or come in for a free initial consultation.
How close is the clinic to Masjid Railway Station?
+
The clinic is a 1-minute walk from Masjid Station on the Harbour Line. Exit towards Bhandari Street and look for Noor Manzil — we're on the 1st floor at number 62.
Do you treat children?
+
Absolutely. Dr. Rangwala is known for being especially patient and gentle with young patients. Many parents bring their children after struggling to find a dentist who could make the experience non-traumatic for kids.
Can I get a same-day appointment?
+
We often have same-day slots available, especially in the morning. Call us at 09870132176 or fill in the booking form above and we'll get back to you within the hour to confirm your slot.
